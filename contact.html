Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

For business partnerships, our minimum order is 50 pieces. For custom orders, we can discuss flexible quantities based on your needs.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we're focused on serving customers within India. International shipping may be available for large orders—please contact us to discuss.

Can I customize products with my brand logo?

Yes! We offer custom branding options including embossed logos, custom finishes, and tailored sizes for business partners.

How long does production take?

Standard orders typically take 3-4 weeks from confirmation. Custom orders may require additional time depending on specifications.

Are your products really dishwasher safe?

Yes! Our products are engineered to withstand regular dishwasher cycles. They're as easy to maintain as traditional tableware.

Can I request a sample before ordering?

Absolutely. We offer sample kits for potential partners. Contact us to request samples and discuss your requirements.
