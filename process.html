How It Works

From farm waste to your table—engineered with precision, crafted with purpose.

Science-Backed Transformation

Our approach is grounded in material science and peer-reviewed research on lignocellulosic biomass valorization.

01

Lignocellulosic Valorization

Research shows that crop residues like rice straw are rich in cellulose and lignin. Instead of burning these valuable polymers, we extract and bind them to create structural integrity.

Scientific Basis:

  • High cellulose content provides tensile strength
  • Lignin acts as a natural binder
  • Reference: "Valorization of crop residues for bio-products" (General Field Study)
02

Thermal Stability Engineering

A key challenge with natural fibers is heat degradation. Our process uses specific pressure-heat cycles to enhance the thermal stability of the composite, making it safe for 120°C beverages.

Engineering Focus:

  • Optimizing glass transition temperature (Tg)
  • Enhancing hydrophobic properties
  • Goal: Durable, dishwasher-safe utility
03

Closed-Loop Manufacturing

We follow principles of the Circular Economy. Waste from one industry (agriculture) becomes the raw material for another (lifestyle goods), with zero toxic runoff.

Sustainability Metrics:

  • Carbon-negative supply chain potential
  • Zero microplastic shedding
  • Biodegradable end-of-life
